About the Artist

The Abstract American is the work of a multi-media artist exploring the intersection of color, form, and emotion. Each piece is a unique journey rendered in acrylic, watercolor, and gouache.

My work is driven by intuition and a deep connection to the materials. The goal is not just to create an image, but to create an experience. This digital gallery serves as a portfolio and an invitation to connect with the art on a personal level.

Commissions & Inquiries

I am currently available for mural projects and custom painting commissions. If my style resonates with you and you have a vision for a unique piece for your home or business, I would love to hear from you.
