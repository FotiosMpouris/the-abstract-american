About

The Abstract American is an evolving gallery of vibrant PNG artworks with a sci-fi/steampunk pulse. It’s meant to feel alive — neon edges, metallic reflections, and a gentle holographic shimmer. Scroll, click, and talk about what you feel. That’s the point here: conversation over commerce.

The site is intentionally light and responsive, designed to showcase art crisply on phones, tablets, and desktops. New pieces are added regularly — keep an eye on the carousel.
