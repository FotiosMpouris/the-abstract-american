About

Art is not a simple creation; it is an exorcism. It's the process of sorting through the difficult, beautiful stages of love and hate as a piece comes together, managing through chaos to find what is true.

Each work is a finished accomplishment, a journey through the challenging and the sublime. This is the core of The Abstract American: not just images on a canvas, but relics of a process, presented for those who feel the same pull.
